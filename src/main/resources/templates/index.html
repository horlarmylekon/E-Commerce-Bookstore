About Us

Ours is a noble responsibility: that of nourishing the minds of our readers with endless knowledge. For close to 10 years, this has been our chief goal. We pride ourselves in having an unrivaled range of books that are rare to find in Nigeria or in the region. We are your go-to bookstore whether you are looking for an interesting novel, or a provocative local memoir.

Our Goal

Supporting the breeding of well groomed children with sound academic foundation well blended with sublime character add value to humanity and it dynamic.

Our Vision

To be a one stop shop in Nigeria providing the most fantastic and very rich learning resources for young learners.

Our Mission

To facilitate building of academic gaints who in turn constitute national human assets adding value to humanity.
